Manage Classes

The "Manage Classes" tab is the main control screen for setting up your classes and managing your reserves lists.

The main links on this page (on the left) allow you to reactivate a class taught in a previous semester (along with all its readings), create a new class, or edit one of your existing classes through the "advanced management" Edit Class screen.

On the right side of this screen are "Quick Links" to frequently used functions such as sorting, annotating, exporting your materials to Blackboard or other Courseware, and viewing the URLs for your materials so that you may post them on a class web page.
