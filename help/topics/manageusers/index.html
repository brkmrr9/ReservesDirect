Manage Users

The Manage Users tab allows you to do two things: manage your own user profile (name and email address); and add or remove proxies from your classes. Clicking on either the "Add Proxy" or "Delete Proxy" links on this page will take you to a screen that asks you to choose one of your current classes. You will then be taken to the Add/Remove proxy screen. For more about how to use this function, click here.
